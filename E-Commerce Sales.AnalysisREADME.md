# E-commerce Sales Strategy Data Analysis 
##  This project involves a comprehensive Exploratory Data Analysis of online sales transactions.Beyond just writing code, this analysis focuses on solving real-world business problems by identifying revenue drivers, seasonal patterns, and customer purchasing behavior.
---
## Business questions 
### 1.What are our "Cash Cow" categories?
* **Analysis:** Grouped total revenue by `Product_Category`.
* **Insight:** **Electronics** is the clear market leader, contributing the highest revenue.

### 2. Ias there any seasonal trend in our sales?
* **Analysis:** Time-series tracking pf monthly revenue.
* **Insight:** Revenue peaked in **January 2024 ($14,548)** and showed a decline toward the summer.This indicates a need for mid-year promotional campaigns to prevent revenue dips during of-peek months.

### 3. Which products are the top performers?
* **Analysis:** Ranked the top 5 products by revenue,
* **Insight:** Premium items like the **Canon EOS R5 CAMERA** amd **LG OVED TV** are top earners.These "hero products" should be prioritizied in advertsising and cross-selling strategies.

### 4.Which regions should we prioritize for expansion?
* **Analysis:** Revenue distribution across global regions.
* **Insight:** **North America** is the most profitable market ($36.8k), outperforming Asia and Europe.This justifies increasing the ad spend and logistics focus in the North American region.

### 5. What is our customers preffered payment method?
* **Anaylsis:** Share of revenue by `Payment Method`.
* **Insights:** **Credit Cards** are the dominant chocie. To improve user experience, the checkout process must be highly optimized for card payments.

### 6. Are we over-reliant on a few products? Top 10 rule
* **Analysis:** Calculated the percentage of total revenue generated by the top 10% of products.
* **Insight:** The top 10% of products generate **45.5%** of total revenue.While this shows high demand for premium items.It also highlights a potential risk if these spesific supply chains are distrupted.

-----


## Technical Implementation
* **Data Cleaning:** Used `pandas` to strip whitespace, handle column naming (replacing spaces with underscores), and verify data integrity (null/duplicate checks).
* **Feature Engineering:** Converted data strings to `datetime` objects and extracted monthly periods for trend analysis.
* **Data Visualization:** Utilized `Seaborn` and `Matplotlib` to create bar charts, line graphs, and pie charts that translate complex data into readable business insights.
